About me

Welcome to my homepage! I am an incoming Assistant Professor at the Department of AI and Data Science, The University of Hong Kong. I recently obtained my Ph.D. from the Department of EECS, MIT, where it was my great plesure to be advised by Sasha Rakhlin. Prior to MIT, I received my diplome in Math/CS from Ecole Normale Superieure, Paris in 2019 and a B.S. in Math from the University of Science and Technology of China in 2017.

Research Interests

My research primarily focuses on the intersection between machine learning theory and interactive decision making. I am particularly interested in:

  • Statistical learning theory
  • Bandits and reinforcement learning
  • Online learning
Note: I am looking for motivated PhD students to join my research group. If you are interested, please send me an email with your CV and research interests.
